# Danse & CO - Product Definition

## Vision
To provide **Danse & CO**, a dance association in Saint-Michel-Chef-Chef, with a professional, modern, and clean web presence that reflects the elegance and energy of "Danse Sportive."

## Goals
1.  **Professionalism:** Remove "DIY" branding (Wix) and adopt a custom, high-end visual identity.
2.  **Clarity:** Make key information (schedules, prices, locations) easy to find and read.
3.  **Engagement:** Use high-quality visuals and clear calls-to-action (CTAs) to encourage registrations.
4.  **Performance:** A fast, responsive, and SEO-friendly site.

## Target Audience
-   Potential students (all ages) looking for dance classes.
-   Current members needing schedules, news, or event info.
-   Parents looking for activities for their children.

## Key Features
-   **Hero Section:** High-impact visual (image/video) with a primary "Register Now" CTA.
-   **Interactive Schedule:** A clean, filterable table or grid for weekly classes.
-   **Location Cards:** Detailed info for *Le Canopus* and *Salle Caraïbes* with embedded maps.
-   **Instructor Profiles:** Professional bios and photos for the teaching team.
-   **News & Events:** A dynamic section for upcoming association highlights.
-   **Contact Form:** A direct way for users to reach out.

## Visual Identity (Draft)
-   **Style:** Minimalist, plenty of whitespace, elegant typography (e.g., a mix of a clean sans-serif for body and a sophisticated serif or geometric font for headings).
-   **Colors:** Deep charcoal, crisp white, and a vibrant "accent" color (perhaps a gold or deep blue) to signify movement and prestige.
-   **Imagery:** Professional photography showing students and instructors in motion.
