# Track 008 Specification: Contact (Form & Direct Communication)

## Overview
This track focuses on the Contact page, providing a professional way for users to reach out.

## Requirements
1.  **Contact Form:**
    -   Fields: Nom, Email, Objet, Message.
    -   Basic validation.
    -   Success/Error feedback.
2.  **Direct Contact Info:**
    -   Phone number.
    -   Email address.
    -   Mailing address.
    -   Social media links.
3.  **Visual Style:**
    -   Clean, two-column layout (Form / Info).
    -   Consistent with the "Stitch" aesthetic.

## Deliverables
-   `src/pages/Contact.tsx`.
-   `src/components/contact/ContactForm.tsx` and `src/components/contact/ContactForm.module.css`.
-   Integration into `App.tsx`.
