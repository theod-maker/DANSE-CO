# Track 006 Specification: Instructors (Bio & Photo Grid)

## Overview
This track focuses on the Instructors page, introducing the teaching team of Danse & CO.

## Requirements
1.  **Instructor Profile Cards:**
    -   Professional photo placeholders.
    -   Name and specialty (e.g., "Danse Sportive", "Salsa").
    -   Short bio highlighting experience and passion.
2.  **Visual Style:**
    -   Elegant grid layout.
    -   Hover effects to reveal more info or social links.
    -   Clean, consistent typography.

## Deliverables
-   `src/pages/Instructors.tsx`.
-   `src/components/instructors/InstructorCard.tsx` and `src/components/instructors/InstructorCard.module.css`.
-   Integration into `App.tsx`.
