# Track 005 Specification: Locations (Venue Cards & Map Integration)

## Overview
This track focuses on the Locations page, detailing where the dance classes take place: *Le Canopus* and *Salle Caraïbes*.

## Requirements
1.  **Venue Cards:**
    -   Professional photos or illustrations of the venues.
    -   Exact addresses and accessibility info.
    -   Specific features (e.g., "Parquet de danse", "Vue sur mer").
2.  **Map Integration:**
    -   Embedded Google Maps for each location.
    -   "Itinéraire" button for quick navigation.
3.  **Visual Style:**
    -   Clean, grid-based layout.
    -   Consistent with the "Stitch" aesthetic (minimalist cards, clear icons).

## Deliverables
-   `src/pages/Locations.tsx`.
-   `src/components/locations/VenueCard.tsx` and `src/components/locations/VenueCard.module.css`.
-   Integration into `App.tsx`.
