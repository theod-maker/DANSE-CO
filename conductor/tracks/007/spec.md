# Track 007 Specification: Disciplines & Pricing (Info & Tables)

## Overview
This track combines the descriptions of dance styles (Disciplines) and the membership costs (Pricing).

## Requirements
1.  **Disciplines Section:**
    -   Detailed descriptions of: Danse Sportive, Rock & Roll, Salsa Cubaine, West Coast Swing.
    -   Benefits of each style.
2.  **Pricing Section:**
    -   A clean, professional pricing table.
    -   Membership categories (e.g., Couple, Individual, Student).
    -   Annual fees info.
    -   "Adhésion" (Membership fee) details.

## Deliverables
-   `src/pages/Disciplines.tsx` and `src/pages/Pricing.tsx` (or a combined page).
-   `src/components/pricing/PricingTable.tsx`.
-   Integration into `App.tsx`.
